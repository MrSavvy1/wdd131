Website Plan: Simple E-commerce Platform

Site Name

Site Name: SimpleShop

Reason: The name "SimpleShop" reflects the straightforward and user-friendly nature of the e-commerce platform, focusing on simplicity and ease of use for browsing and purchasing products.

Site Purpose

The purpose of SimpleShop is to provide a seamless online shopping experience where users can browse, search for, and purchase products easily. The site will offer detailed product information, high-quality images, and a simulated checkout process to mimic real-world e-commerce functionality.

Scenarios

  • How can I find the latest products and special offers?
  • What are the details and prices of a specific product I'm interested in?

Color Schema

The color scheme for SimpleShop will focus on creating a clean and modern aesthetic:

  • Primary Color: #2c3e50 (Dark Blue) - used for headings, navigation bar, and footer.
  • Secondary Color: #ecf0f1 (Light Gray) - used for background and sections to create contrast.
  • Accent Color: #e74c3c (Red) - used for buttons, highlights, and special offers to draw attention.

Typography

The website will use a combination of modern and readable fonts:

  • Headings: 'Roboto', sans-serif - for a clean and modern look.
  • Body Text: 'Open Sans', sans-serif - for readability and accessibility.
